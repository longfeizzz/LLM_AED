1. Going to school is not exclusively accessible to wealthy parents; other financial aid options exist.
2. Many schools offer scholarships, grants, and financial assistance programs for students from various backgrounds.
3. Government-funded schools and public education systems do not require parental wealth for enrollment.
4. Some private schools offer tuition discounts based on need rather than solely on wealth.
5. Scholarships and merit-based awards can be available to students regardless of their parents' financial status.
