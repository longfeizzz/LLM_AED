1. Tapas are traditionally small portions of food.
2. The name 'tapa' is derived from the practice of serving a small amount of food with a drink, not large portions.
3. The food is served on top of the glass like a lid, suggesting a small amount.
4. The concept of tapas is often associated with social gatherings where small portions are shared among multiple people.
5. Tapas are commonly served as appetizers or snacks, not as a filling meal.
